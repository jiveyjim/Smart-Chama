About Smart Chama

Smart Chama started in 2019 as a neighborhood savings group and has since built a disciplined, transparent, and technology-driven approach to collective saving. We combine group discipline with automated records and clear payout schedules so every member can trust the process.

Our Mission

To empower members to save, access lump-sum capital, and achieve short-term and long-term financial goals through a transparent, auditable system.

Our Values

  • Transparency — open statements for every contribution
  • Fairness — strict withdrawal order and rules
  • Security — audited transaction records and backups

Our Approach

We record every deposit and withdrawal, publish periodic statements, and use an ordered timeline to determine payouts so members know who is next — always.

How Smart Chama Works

  1. Join the Chama

    Register with your full name, phone, email and ID. New members join at the end of the current withdrawal queue.

  2. Daily Contribution

    Make your daily contribution using M-PESA STK push (or manual deposit) — every payment is recorded in the system.

  3. Withdrawal by Turn

    Withdraw only on your assigned date/turn. When you withdraw, you are moved to the back of the queue so everyone gets their turn.

  4. Full Transparency

    Statements show every deposit and withdrawal. Admin announcements and emails keep members informed of meetings and changes.

Features & Benefits

Automated Contributions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can withdraw?

The member whose position is first in the withdrawal timeline and whose scheduled date it is. The system enforces this rule.

How are new members placed in the timeline?

New members are added to the end of the queue. Admin adding members will automatically update the positions.

What happens after a member withdraws?

They are moved to the back of the queue and their withdrawal is recorded as a transaction for audit purposes.
